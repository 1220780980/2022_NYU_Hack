Global Warming

Main Causes of Global Warming

  1. Soaring population
  2. Air pollution
  3. Water contamination
  4. Reduced forest resources

Consequences of Global Warming

  1. Rising sea level
  2. Heatwave
  3. Drought
  4. Loss of Biodiversity
  5. Wildfire & Desertification

Simple Ways to Reduce Global Warming

  1. Recyling & Reuse
  2. Garbage sorting
  3. Biological liquid fuel
  4. Use paper/cloth bags
